Chapter 1. Session Manager Guide

1.1. Session Manager

Being a Session Manager you have full control of your session. However your conference manager may request you not to edit some parts of the session. You are given the session manager rights by your conference manager.

To access the management area of your session, use the Timetable option on the left menu and click on your session's title, if you have been assigned as the session manager you will see a small red pen icon in the top right this will take you to your session.
