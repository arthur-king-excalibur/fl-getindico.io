Chapter 1. Submitters Guide

1.1. Submitter

Being a submitter you are able to submit material for a contribution. Your are given submitter access by either the Conference Manager, Session Manager, Contribution Manager or possibly the Session Co-ordinator.

You can access the contributions you can submit to by selecting 'my contributions' from the left hand menu under my conference, this will take you to your submission area and will list the contributions you have access to submit material for.
